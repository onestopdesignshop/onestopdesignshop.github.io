Yes. All assets come with a commercial license. Use them in client work, products you sell, apps, websites — no attribution needed.

Icons: SVG, PNG (1x, 2x, 3x), Figma component file. Fonts: OTF, TTF, WOFF2, variable font. Mockups: PSD, Figma smart objects.

Bundle buyers get lifetime updates. Individual pack buyers get 12 months of updates. All downloads are accessible forever through your Gumroad library.

We offer a 14-day refund if you're not happy, no questions asked. Just email us with your order number.
Can I share files with my team?
Each license covers one person. Teams of 5+ can contact us for a studio license at a discounted rate.
